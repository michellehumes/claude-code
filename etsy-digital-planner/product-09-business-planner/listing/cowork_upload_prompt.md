### TROUBLESHOOTING

**If images appear blurry:**
- Ensure images are 2400x2400px minimum
- Re-upload in JPG format at 95% quality

**If the ZIP file is too large:**
- Etsy allows up to 20MB per digital file
- If over limit, split into multiple ZIP files

**If formulas do not work for a customer:**
- Advise them to open in Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets
- Some mobile apps have limited formula support
- Suggest using desktop/laptop for full functionality

**If a customer requests Google Sheets format:**
- Advise them to upload the .xlsx file to Google Drive
- Right-click > Open with > Google Sheets
- All formulas and formatting will convert automatically
