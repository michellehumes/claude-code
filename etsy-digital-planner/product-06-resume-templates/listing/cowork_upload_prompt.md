### Step 1: Start New Listing

1. Log in to your Etsy Seller account at **etsy.com/sell**
2. Navigate to **Shop Manager** > **Listings**
3. Click **Add a listing** (top right)
4. Select **Digital** as the listing type

### Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Images look blurry | Ensure originals are 2400x2400px, re-upload at full resolution |
| Video won't upload | Confirm MP4 format, under 100MB, max 15 seconds |
| Digital files too large | Compress PNGs or convert to high-quality JPG |
| Tags not saving | Ensure each tag is under 20 characters |
| Listing not appearing in search | Allow 24-48 hours for indexing, verify tags match search terms |
