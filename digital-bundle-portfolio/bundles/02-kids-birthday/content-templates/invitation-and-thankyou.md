## Font Pairing

- **"Wild" / "ONE" / Name:** Fredoka One (playful, rounded bold)
- **Event details:** Nunito Regular, 11pt
- **Body text:** Nunito Light, 10pt
- **Accent labels:** Lora Italic, 9pt

## Color Application

- Child's name: Terracotta (#C67B5C)
- Event headers: Dark Brown (#4A3728)
- Body text: Dark Brown (#4A3728)
- Background: Warm Cream (#FDF6EE)
- Border/accents: Warm Sage (#A8B89C) + Golden Sand (#E8C87A)
- Illustration style: Watercolor animals with soft edges

## Print Specs

**Invitation:**
- Size: 5x7 (standard) and 4x6 (budget)
- Single or double-sided
- Paper: 100lb cardstock
- Envelope: A7 (for 5x7)

**Thank You:**
- Size: 5x7 flat or A2 folded
- Paper: 100lb cardstock

**Digital:**
- 1080x1080 px, RGB, 72 DPI
- Optimized for text message, email, social share

## Mockup Direction

- Flat lay on kraft paper with tropical leaf
- Small toy safari animal beside it
- Warm, playful lighting
- Confetti dots scattered around

**Thank You:**
- Held in hand against blurred party backdrop
- Or flat lay with party favor and animal figurine
