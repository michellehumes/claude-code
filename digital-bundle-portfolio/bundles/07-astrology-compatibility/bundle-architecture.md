## Design Specifications

### Color Palette
- Primary: Deep Midnight (#2B2D42)
- Secondary: Dusty Lavender (#B8A9C9)
- Accent: Muted Gold (#C9AE5D)
- Tertiary: Soft Rose (#D4A0A0)
- Text: Off-White (#F0EBE3) on dark / Charcoal (#3A3A3A) on light
- Background: Deep Navy + Warm Cream alternating

### Typography
- Headers: Celestial Serif (Cinzel or equivalent)
- Body: Clean Sans-Serif (Outfit Light or equivalent)
- Accent: Mystical script for sign names (Corinthia or equivalent)

### Visual Elements
- Constellation line art and star field backgrounds
- Zodiac glyphs as design accents (not clip art)
- Celestial motifs: moon phases, stars, planetary symbols
- Watercolor nebula washes (subtle, not overwhelming)
- Gold foil effect accents (printed as muted gold tones)
- Clean layouts with generous white/dark space
- NO cartoon zodiac characters. NO cheesy astrology imagery.

### File Formats
- **PDF:** 300 DPI, print-ready
- **Canva:** Editable links for cover page and customizable pieces
- **JPG:** For phone wallpapers and social media pieces
- Home-printer optimized sizes

## Personalization System

### How It Works (Manual Customization)

**Buyer selects their sign pairing** from listing variations or add instructions.

**Option A — Pre-Built Pairings (Phase 1):**
- Launch with 12 most popular sign pairings (e.g., Aries+Libra, Leo+Sagittarius)
- Each is a ready-to-download kit

**Option B — Universal Kit with Swap Pages (Phase 1 Alternative):**
- Sell one universal kit with all 12 sign profiles included
- Buyer selects their two signs and assembles
- Lower production cost, slightly lower perceived personalization

**Option C — Full 144 Pairing Library (Phase 2):**
- Automated template system generates all 144 combinations
- Maximum perceived personalization
- Premium pricing at $39+ per pairing
