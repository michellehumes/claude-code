## FAQ Section

**Q: What sizes are the wall art prints?**
A: Every wall art print comes in 4 sizes: 5x7, 8x10, 11x14, and 16x20. Select gallery compositions show recommended size arrangements.

**Q: Can I really reuse these every year?**
A: Absolutely. These are digital files — yours forever. Print new pieces each year or rotate your favorites. Many customers frame a core set and swap accent pieces annually.

**Q: What printer do I need?**
A: Any color inkjet or laser printer works for pieces up to 11x14. For 16x20 prints, upload to a professional service (Walmart, Costco, Shutterfly, or a local print shop). Our included guide has printer setting recommendations.

**Q: Are the place cards and menus editable?**
A: Yes. All text-heavy pieces include Canva template links for full editing. PDF versions have pre-designed layouts for direct printing.

**Q: What paper should I use for wall art?**
A: Matte photo paper or heavyweight cardstock for best results. Our framing guide includes specific paper recommendations for each piece type.

**Q: Is this a physical product?**
A: No. Digital download only. Instant access after purchase.
