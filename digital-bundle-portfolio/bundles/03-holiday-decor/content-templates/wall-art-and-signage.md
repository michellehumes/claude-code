## Font Pairing

- **Art print titles:** Playfair Display (serif) or Sacramento (script)
- **Signage headers:** Playfair Display Bold, proportional to sign
- **Signage body:** Raleway Light, 50–60% of header size
- **Gift tags:** Raleway Regular, small scale
- **Accent text:** Sacramento (script) for key words only

## Color Application

- Typography prints: Mix of Sage (#B5C4B1), Gold (#C7A95B), Charcoal (#3D3D3D)
- Signage: Charcoal text, sage accents, gold dividers
- Gift tags: Charcoal text, sage/gold illustrations
- All backgrounds: Off-White (#FAFAF7) or Warm Cream (#F5EDE0)

## Print Specs

**Wall Art:**
- Sizes per print: 5x7, 8x10, 11x14, 16x20
- Resolution: 300 DPI
- Color: sRGB + CMYK variants
- Format: PDF + JPG

**Signage:**
- Size: 8x10 standard (some 5x7)
- Resolution: 300 DPI
- Format: PDF

**Gift Tags:**
- Size: 2x3 inches, 6 per sheet on 8.5x11
- Include cut lines
- Format: PDF

## Mockup Direction

**Wall Art:**
- Gallery wall arrangement on neutral wall
- Simple wood or gold frames
- Styled shelf with candle and greenery
- Living room or bedroom setting

**Signage:**
- Framed on countertop near activity station
- Kitchen or dining room setting
- Candles, mugs, evergreen sprigs nearby

**Gift Tags:**
- Attached to kraft paper wrapped gifts
- Twine or ribbon
- Under tree or on wrapping table
- Natural lighting, warm tones
