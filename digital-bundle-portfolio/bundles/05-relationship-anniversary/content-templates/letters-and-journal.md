## Couples Journal Prompts (Bonus Section)

### 30 Guided Prompts

**Getting Started:**
1. What's your earliest memory of your partner?
2. When did you first know this was something special?
3. What were your first impressions of each other?

**Going Deeper:**
4. What does your partner do that makes you feel most loved?
5. When have you felt most proud of your relationship?
6. What challenge strengthened your bond?
7. What's a compromise you're glad you made?
8. How has your partner changed you for the better?

**Having Fun:**
9. What's the funniest thing that's happened to you as a couple?
10. What's a secret talent your partner has?
11. What would your couple reality show be called?
12. What's your partner's most endearing quirk?

**Looking Ahead:**
13. Where do you see yourselves in 5 years?
14. What adventure do you want to take together?
15. What tradition do you want to start?
16. What does "growing old together" look like to you?

**Appreciation:**
17. What does your partner do that nobody else notices?
18. What's the best advice your partner has given you?
19. How does your partner make ordinary days special?
20. What quality of your partner's do you hope your children inherit?

**Vulnerability:**
21. What's something you've never told your partner?
22. When do you feel most connected?
23. What's your greatest fear about the future — and how does your partner ease it?
24. What do you wish you did more of together?

**Celebration:**
25. What's the best decision you've made as a couple?
26. What's your favorite "us" memory from this year?
27. What song is "your song" and why?
28. What's the best gift your partner has given you?
29. How do you want to celebrate your next milestone?
30. If you wrote your love story, what would the title be?

## Universal Design Specs

### Color Palette
- Background: Warm Ivory (#FBF7F2)
- Text: Dark Espresso (#3B302A)
- Lines: Light Taupe (#D5CCC3)
- Accents: Muted Gold (#C4A55A), Soft Blush (#E8D5CC)

### Typography
- Letter headers: Bodoni Moda Italic, 28pt
- Greetings: Pinyon Script, 18pt
- Prompts: Jost Light Italic, 10pt (in lighter color)
- Writing lines: Jost Light, 11pt baseline
- Closing: Jost Regular, 11pt

### Print Specs
- Standard: 8.5x11 for letters and journal pages
- Cards: 3x4 (reasons), 5x7 (open when)
- All 300 DPI, PDF format
- Paper recommendation: Quality text weight, ivory or cream
