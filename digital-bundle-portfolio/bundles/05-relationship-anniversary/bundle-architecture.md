## Design Specifications

### Color Palette
- Primary: Warm Taupe (#B8A99A)
- Secondary: Soft Blush (#E8D5CC)
- Accent: Muted Gold (#C4A55A)
- Text: Dark Espresso (#3B302A)
- Background: Warm Ivory (#FBF7F2)

### Typography
- Headers: Romantic Serif (Bodoni Moda or equivalent)
- Body: Modern Sans-Serif (Jost Light or equivalent)
- Accent: Elegant script for "Love," names (Pinyon Script or equivalent)

### Visual Elements
- Subtle watercolor washes in warm tones
- Delicate line art — intertwined rings, botanical, hearts (tasteful, not childish)
- Photography-forward layouts — generous photo spaces
- Texture: linen, handmade paper feel in backgrounds
- NO clip art. NO cartoon couples. NO cliché imagery.

### File Formats
- **PDF:** 300 DPI, print-ready
- **Canva:** Editable links for all personalization-heavy pieces
- Home-printer optimized (8.5x11, A4, or standard card sizes)

## "12-Month Relationship Challenge" Add-On Detail

This is the signature upsell differentiator for this vertical.

**Concept:** A structured year-long couples growth journey with monthly themes, weekly micro-challenges, and reflection checkpoints.

**Month Themes:**
1. **Gratitude** — Daily appreciation practices
2. **Communication** — Active listening + expression exercises
3. **Adventure** — Try something new together each week
4. **Nostalgia** — Revisit meaningful places and memories
5. **Service** — Acts of love and care for each other
6. **Vulnerability** — Deeper sharing and emotional openness
7. **Play** — Fun, humor, lightness together
8. **Growth** — Individual goals supported as a team
9. **Intimacy** — Connection rituals (emotional + physical)
10. **Dreams** — Shared future visioning
11. **Resilience** — Reflecting on challenges overcome
12. **Celebration** — Honoring the journey and each other

**Standalone sale potential:** This challenge can be extracted as its own $19–$29 product.
