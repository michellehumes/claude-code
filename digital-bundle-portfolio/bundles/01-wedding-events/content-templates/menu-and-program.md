## Font Pairing (Menus & Programs)

- **Couple names / Titles:** Playlist Script or Cormorant Garamond Bold Italic
- **Course headers:** Cormorant Garamond Bold, Small Caps
- **Dish names:** Montserrat Regular, 11pt
- **Descriptions:** Montserrat Light Italic, 9pt
- **Body (program):** Montserrat Light, 10pt, 1.5 line height

## Color Application

- Title/names: Muted Gold (#C9A96E)
- Course headers: Dusty Blue (#A4C3D2)
- Body text: Charcoal (#3A3A3A)
- Rules/dividers: Sand (#E8DDD3)
- Background: Warm White (#FAF8F5)

## Print Specs

**Menu Cards:**
- Sizes: 4x9 (slim) or 5x7 (standard)
- Single-sided
- Paper: 100lb text or 80lb cardstock

**Ceremony Program:**
- Size: 5x7 (flat) or 8.5x11 folded to 5.5x8.5 (bi-fold)
- Paper: 80lb text, white or cream

## Mockup Direction

- Soft candlelight

**Program:**
