# Content Template: Welcome Letter — Coastal Wedding Weekend

## Layout Hierarchy

1. **Header:** Couple's names in script + date
2. **Greeting:** "Welcome to [Location]!"
3. **Body:** 3–4 paragraphs (personal, logistical, gratitude)
4. **Footer:** Couple's names + wedding hashtag

## Written Content Template

---

**[First Name] & [First Name]**
*[Month Day, Year]*

---

**Welcome to [Location]!**

We are so thrilled you're here to celebrate with us. Having you as part of our wedding weekend means the world to us — truly, this celebration wouldn't be the same without you.

We've put together this little guide to help you make the most of the weekend. Inside you'll find the schedule, some of our favorite local spots, and everything you need to know to relax and enjoy the next few days.

Our only request: be present, have fun, and soak in every moment. The ocean breeze, the good company, and the love in the air — that's what this weekend is all about.

We can't wait to celebrate with you.

With love and gratitude,
**[First Name] & [First Name]**

*#[WeddingHashtag]*

---

## Font Pairing

- **Names/Header:** Cormorant Garamond Bold (or Playfair Display)
- **Body:** Montserrat Light, 11pt, 1.6 line height
- **Hashtag/Footer:** Montserrat Regular Italic, 9pt

## Color Application

- Header text: Muted Gold (#C9A96E)
- Body text: Charcoal (#3A3A3A)
- Background: Warm White (#FAF8F5)
- Decorative border: Dusty Blue (#A4C3D2), thin watercolor wash at top

## Print Specs

- **Size:** 8.5 x 11 inches (letter) and A4
- **Margins:** 1.25" all sides
- **Bleed:** None (home printing)
- **Paper recommendation:** 80lb text weight, cream or white

## Mockup Direction

- Flat lay on linen tablecloth
- Paired with eucalyptus sprig and kraft envelope
- Warm, natural lighting from top-left
- Beach sand or shell accent in corner
