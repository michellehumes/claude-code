## Design Specifications

### Color Palette
- Primary: Dusty Blue (#A4C3D2)
- Secondary: Sand/Warm Beige (#E8DDD3)
- Accent: Muted Gold (#C9A96E)
- Text: Charcoal (#3A3A3A)
- Background: Warm White (#FAF8F5)

### Typography
- Headers: Modern Serif (Cormorant Garamond or equivalent)
- Body: Clean Sans-Serif (Montserrat Light or equivalent)
- Accent: Script for names only (Playlist Script or equivalent)

### Visual Elements
- Watercolor coastal washes (subtle, corners/edges)
- Delicate line-art shells, coral, eucalyptus
- No clip art. No cartoons. No heavy illustrations.
- White space dominant. Elegant minimalism.

### File Formats
- **PDF:** Print-ready, 300 DPI, CMYK, with trim marks where applicable
- **Canva:** Editable template links for text-heavy pieces
- Size variations included for each piece as specified above
