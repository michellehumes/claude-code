## FAQ Section

**Q: Do I need Canva Pro?**
A: No. All Canva templates work with a free Canva account. However, some premium fonts may require substitution on the free plan — we include alternative font recommendations.

**Q: Can I change the colors?**
A: The PDF files are print-ready in the coastal palette shown. The Canva templates allow you to change colors, fonts, and all text.

**Q: What paper should I use?**
A: We recommend 80–100lb cardstock for signs and cards, and standard 80lb text weight for programs and letters. Our included guide has detailed paper recommendations for every piece.

**Q: Can I print these at home?**
A: Yes. All pieces up to 11x14 are optimized for home printing. For larger signs (16x20+), we recommend uploading to a print service like FedEx, Staples, or an online printer.

**Q: How many guests can the seating chart accommodate?**
A: The seating chart template supports up to 200 guests. Table numbers go up to 30.

**Q: Can I get a custom color palette?**
A: Yes! We offer a Concierge Customization Package. Message us for details. Basic name/date customization starts at $25. Full custom palette + text: $99.

**Q: Is this a physical product?**
A: No. This is a digital download. No physical items will be shipped. You'll receive instant access after purchase.
