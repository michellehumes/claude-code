## How to Set Up Shop Sections on Etsy

1. Go to **Shop Manager** → **Listings**
2. Click the **gear icon** next to "Sections" in the left sidebar
3. Click **Add a section**
4. Enter the section name (keep it short — max ~24 characters for clean display)
5. Drag sections to reorder (put Villa Vibes sections at the top)
6. Assign each listing to its section by editing the listing → "Section" dropdown

### Recommended Section Order (top to bottom):
1. Villa Vibes Bundles ← hero products, highest AOV
2. Villa Vibes Water Bottles ← most searched product
3. Villa Vibes Party Pack ← digital download upsell
4. Wedding & Event Stationery
5. Digital Planners & Spreadsheets

**Why this order:** Visitors to your shop land on the main page and see sections from top to bottom. Leading with bundles puts your highest-value offering first. Water bottles (physical + personalised) are the most searchable. Party pack is the natural upsell after seeing the bottles.

Update your Etsy shop banner to reflect the Villa Vibes brand:

- **Design:** Clean layout with "Villa Vibes" in cursive script, a few bottles displayed, and text like "Personalised Bottles | Party Packs | Bachelorette Essentials"
