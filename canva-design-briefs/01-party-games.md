## Game 5: "The Compatibility Quiz"

**Canva document size:** 8.5 x 11" (US Letter)
**Total pages:** 3 (instructions + groom's sheet + bride's sheet)

### Page 1: Instructions

Header: `THE COMPATIBILITY QUIZ` (Bebas Neue, 44pt, Villa Pink)
Subheader: `HOW WELL DO THEY REALLY KNOW EACH OTHER?` (Montserrat Bold, 14pt, Coral Pop)

**Before the party:** Send the groom the quiz (Page 2). He fills it out and
sends his answers back to the MOH. Do NOT let the bride see.

**At the party:** Give the bride the bride's version (Page 3). She answers
each question. Then the MOH reveals the groom's answers one at a time.

**Scoring:**
- Exact match = 2 points
- Close enough (group decides) = 1 point
- Total mismatch = 0 points (but probably funny)
- 16-20 points: Soulmates. 10-15: Solid. Under 10: Yikes (but entertaining).

5. What is her go-to comfort food?
6. What would she say is your best quality?
7. What is the one thing you two always argue about?
8. Describe her perfect Sunday in 5 words
9. What's the most romantic thing you've done for her?
10. If you could relive one moment together, which would it be?

### Page 3: Bride's Answer Sheet

Same 10 questions but framed for her:
