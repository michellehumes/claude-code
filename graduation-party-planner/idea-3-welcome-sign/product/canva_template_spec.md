# Graduation Party Welcome Sign — Canva Template Specification

## Overview
An editable Canva template for a graduation party welcome sign.
Buyers receive the Canva template link. They edit the text directly in Canva,
download as PDF or PNG, and print at home or at a print shop.

---

## Canvas Dimensions
- **Size:** 18 × 24 inches (portrait poster format)
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI (high quality for printing)
- **Color Mode:** CMYK-friendly (RGB in Canva, optimized for print)

---

## Editable Sections (buyers customize these)

| Field | Example Value | Instructions |
|---|---|---|
| Graduate Name | Emily Johnson | Replace in headline |
| Graduation Year | Class of 2026 | Replace in subheading |
| School Name | Westfield High School | Replace in body text |
| Party Date | June 14, 2026 | Replace in detail line |
| Welcome Message | "Welcome to my Graduation Party!" | Replace in main message |

---

## Design Variants Included

### Variant A — Elegant Navy & Gold
- Background: Deep navy (`#1a2744`)
- Headline font: Serif (e.g., Cormorant Garamond Bold), Gold (`#D4AF37`)
- Body font: Sans-serif (e.g., Montserrat), White
- Decorative elements: Gold diploma ribbon, laurel wreath border, corner flourishes
- Best for: Formal, traditional graduation parties

### Variant B — Modern White & Pink
- Background: White (`#ffffff`) with blush pink (`#fb5887`) accents
- Headline font: Bold condensed sans (e.g., Bebas Neue), Dark (`#2C3E50`)
- Subheading: Script accent font (e.g., Dancing Script), Pink
- Decorative elements: Confetti dots, graduation cap icon, thin pink border
- Best for: Fun, modern, youthful celebration vibe

---

## Design Elements

### Structural Layout (top to bottom)
1. **Top decorative band** — color stripe or pattern strip (20px)
2. **Welcome heading** — "WELCOME TO" (small caps, 48pt)
3. **Graduate's Name** — Large hero text (120pt bold) — *Primary editable field*
4. **Divider line** — thin gold or pink rule
5. **Celebration text** — "GRADUATION PARTY" (72pt)
6. **School name & year** — "Westfield High School · Class of 2026" (32pt)
7. **Decorative element** — graduation cap or wreath illustration
8. **Party date line** — "Saturday, June 14, 2026" (28pt, italic)
9. **Optional message line** — "Please sign the guest book!" (22pt)
10. **Bottom decorative band** — matching top stripe

### Fonts Used (Canva built-in, free)
- Cormorant Garamond Bold — headline (Variant A)
- Bebas Neue — headline (Variant B)
- Montserrat SemiBold — subheadings
- Dancing Script — script accent
- Montserrat Regular — body details

## How Buyers Use This Template

1. Purchase on Etsy → receive Canva template link via Etsy message or download PDF instructions
2. Click the link → opens in Canva (free Canva account required)
3. Click "Use Template" to get their own editable copy
4. Edit: Replace name, year, school, date, message
5. Download as PDF (Print) or PNG at 300 DPI
6. Print at home or upload to a local print shop
