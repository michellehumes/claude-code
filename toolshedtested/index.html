How We Test Every Tool

Our 5-step testing methodology ensures consistent, reliable ratings you can trust.

1

Purchase at Retail

We buy every tool at full retail price from major retailers. No manufacturer samples, no freebies, no strings attached.

2

Standardized Benchmarks

Each tool category has defined test protocols. Drills get torque tests, runtime measurements, and drilling counts. Saws get cut-speed timing, accuracy checks, and blade deflection tests.

3

Real-World Projects

Beyond benchmarks, every tool is used on actual jobsite projects — framing, trim, demo, and finish work. We evaluate how tools perform under sustained, realistic workloads.

4

Long-Term Durability

Tools stay in our rotation for 3-6 months. We monitor battery degradation, switch reliability, gear wear, and overall build quality over hundreds of hours of use.

5

Scoring & Ranking

Final ratings are weighted: Performance (30%), Durability (25%), Ergonomics (20%), Value (15%), Features (10%). Every score is backed by specific, documented test results.
