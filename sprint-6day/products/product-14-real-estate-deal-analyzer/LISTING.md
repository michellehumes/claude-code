## Description
Stop guessing if a deal is good. Know in 5 minutes.

This Real Estate Deal Analyzer tells you exactly what every investment property is worth — cash-on-cash return, cap rate, monthly cash flow, ARV, 70% rule check — automatically, the moment you enter the numbers.

Built for investors who evaluate 5+ deals before finding one worth buying.

✅ **5 tabs. Everything an investor needs:**

**🏘 Deal Analyzer**
Enter the property details, and every metric calculates instantly:
- Monthly & annual cash flow
- Cash-on-Cash Return % (target: 8%+)
- Cap Rate %
- Gross Rent Multiplier (GRM)
- Net Operating Income (NOI)
- Monthly P&I payment (via PMT formula — just enter rate + term)
- ARV equity & 70% ARV rule check for flips
- **Deal Score** — automatically rates the deal: 🟢 Strong / 🟡 Marginal / 🔴 Pass

**🔍 Deal Comparison**
Compare up to 5 properties side-by-side. Same metrics across every deal so you can pick the best one without re-doing the math.

**🔨 Rehab Estimator**
25 common rehab line items pre-loaded with realistic low/high cost ranges: kitchen, bathroom, flooring, roof, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, and more. Enter your estimate, total calculates automatically.

**📈 10-Year Rental Projections**
Set your starting rent, annual rent increase %, and expense growth rate. Projects 10 years of cash flow and cumulative returns — so you can see the long-term picture before you buy.

**📖 Setup Guide**
Explains every metric (CoC, Cap Rate, GRM, ARV) in plain English. Includes the 70% rule formula and how to use it.

✅ **Who it's for:**
- New investors analyzing their first rental property
- BRRRR investors running numbers before offers
- House flippers checking the 70% ARV rule
- Anyone tired of doing real estate math by hand
