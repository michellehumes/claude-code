## Description
Renovating is exciting. Going $20,000 over budget is not.

This Home Renovation Budget Tracker gives you complete control over your project — room by room, dollar by dollar — so you always know where your money is going before it disappears into a dumpster.

✅ **5 tabs. Everything your renovation needs:**

**🏠 Dashboard**
Real-time overview: total budget vs. total spent, rooms in progress, and an alert banner that tells you immediately if you're heading over budget.

**📋 Room-by-Room Budget Planner**
Add every room or project area. Set your budget for labor + materials separately. Track contractor names, project status, and payment schedule. Total budget auto-calculates.

**🔨 Contractor Bid Comparison**
Enter up to 30 bids side-by-side. Compare contractor name, bid amount, what's included, license number, and references. The lowest bid highlights automatically.

**💳 Detailed Cost Tracker**
Log every single purchase — lumber, fixtures, permit fees, the $400 tile you impulse-bought. Category dropdowns included: Labor, Materials, Fixtures, Appliances, Permits, and more.

**📖 Setup Guide**
Plain-English instructions. No spreadsheet experience needed.

✅ **Includes the 15% Contingency Calculator**
Renovations almost always run over. This tracker builds in a 15% contingency buffer and shows you exactly how much to set aside before you start.

**Works in:** Google Sheets (free) + Microsoft Excel. Both formats included in your download.
