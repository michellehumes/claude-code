## Description
Less paperwork. More therapy.

This Therapy Practice Management Kit is a complete Google Sheets system built specifically for licensed therapists, counselors, social workers, and coaches — so you can spend less time on admin and more time with your clients.

✅ **6 tabs. Your entire practice in one file:**

**📊 Practice Dashboard**
At a glance: active clients, sessions this month, revenue, outstanding balances, cancellation rate, and average session rate. No more digging through spreadsheets to answer basic questions.

**👥 Client Roster**
Up to 200 clients. Tracks: client ID, contact info, insurance, diagnosis code, session rate, preferred schedule, emergency contact. Status dropdown: Active / Inactive / Waitlist / Discharged.

**📅 Session Log**
Log every session in seconds. Client ID, date, session type (Individual / Couples / Group / Crisis), status (Completed / Cancelled / No-Show), notes, next session date, and homework assigned.

**💰 Billing & Payment Tracker**
Every invoice in one place. Track payment status (Paid / Unpaid / Partial / Insurance Pending / Waived), payment method, insurance claim numbers, and date paid. Outstanding balance calculates automatically.

**📈 Caseload Overview**
One-page practice summary: active clients, total sessions, no-show rate, monthly revenue, outstanding balances, YTD revenue. Perfect for monthly business reviews.

**📖 Setup Guide**
Step-by-step instructions including privacy best practices for storing client data in Google Drive.

✅ **Privacy-conscious design:**
Includes a setup guide covering how to restrict Google Drive sharing to protect client PHI. Built for private practice — not corporate EMR systems.

**Works in:** Google Sheets only (recommended for privacy + free access). Excel version included if preferred.

**Who it's for:**
- Solo private practice therapists (LCSWs, LPCs, LMFTs, psychologists)
- Mental health counselors
- Life coaches tracking client programs
- Any practitioner managing a caseload under 200
