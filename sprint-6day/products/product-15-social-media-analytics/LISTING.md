## Description
Stop posting into the void. Start tracking what actually works.

This Social Media Content Calendar & Analytics Tracker is the only spreadsheet you need to plan, schedule, post, and analyze your content — across every platform — in one place.

✅ **6 tabs. Your entire content operation:**

**📊 Dashboard**
Real-time snapshot: posts this month, average engagement rate, total followers, follower growth, and scheduled posts waiting to go live. Updates automatically as you log data.

**📅 Content Calendar (1,000 rows)**
Plan everything: platform, date, content type (Reel / Carousel / Static / Story / Pin / etc.), full caption/hook, hashtags, visual description, CTA, and link. Status dropdown: Idea → In Progress → Scheduled → Posted.

**📈 Analytics Log**
After each post goes live, log your likes, comments, shares/saves, and reach. The **Engagement Rate calculates automatically**: (Likes + Comments + Shares) ÷ Reach. No math required.

**Platform Averages Table** at the bottom of the Analytics Log — automatically calculates your average engagement rate, likes, reach, and total posts per platform. See in 5 seconds which platform is actually working for you.

**📊 Follower Growth Tracker**
Current followers + monthly goal + % to goal for every platform. Plus a 12-month rolling log to see your growth trajectory over time.

**# Hashtag Bank**
Pre-loaded with 17 researched hashtags across small business, Etsy, digital products, finance, and creator niches. Rate each by performance. Add your own. Stop searching for hashtags every time you post.

**📖 Setup Guide**
Includes the 80/20 rule for content: how to identify your top 20% of posts and do more of what's already working.
