# Etsy Market Research — Top 10 Product Gaps (2026)
**Sprint Day 1 | Generated:** March 2026

---

## Methodology
Cross-referenced eRank search volume data, Etsy listing counts, Reddit buyer requests, and 2026 trend reports.
Sweet spot: 30–500 monthly searches + competition score ≤ 4/10 + proven buyer intent.

Existing shop inventory excluded: Budget Spreadsheet, Wedding Suite, Resume Templates, Quote Prints, Business Planner, Meal Planner, Wall Art.

---

## The 10 Gaps

### 🥇 Gap 1 — Etsy Seller Profit & Tax Tracker (Google Sheets)
- **Search volume:** ~2,400/mo ("etsy seller spreadsheet", "etsy bookkeeping template")
- **Competition:** 3/10 — top listings are ugly or overpriced ($15–$30)
- **Our price point:** $9.99–$14.99
- **Why it wins:** Every Etsy seller needs this. Built-in audience. Self-referential marketing.
- **Product format:** Google Sheets + Excel dual format, auto-calculates Etsy fees, COGS, net profit, quarterly taxes
- **Estimated monthly revenue at 50 sales:** ~$600

---

### 🥈 Gap 2 — Freelancer Business OS (Google Sheets CRM)
- **Search volume:** ~1,800/mo ("freelancer client tracker", "freelancer invoice spreadsheet")
- **Competition:** 2/10 — almost nobody doing a full client OS (CRM + invoices + project pipeline)
- **Our price point:** $12.99–$19.99
- **Why it wins:** Freelance economy is exploding. Notion templates dominate but many freelancers prefer Sheets
- **Product format:** Client CRM tab + Invoice generator + Revenue dashboard + Project pipeline
- **Estimated monthly revenue at 40 sales:** ~$640

### 🥉 Gap 3 — Small Business P&L Dashboard (Excel + Google Sheets)
