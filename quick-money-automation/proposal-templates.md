## CUSTOMIZATION CHECKLIST
Before sending each proposal:
- [ ] Replace ALL [bracketed] placeholders
- [ ] Mention specific detail from job posting
- [ ] Adjust price based on complexity ($50-100 range)
- [ ] Include realistic timeline (1-3 hours max)
- [ ] Check spelling/grammar
- [ ] Keep it under 150 words

## PRICING GUIDE FOR RUSH WORK
- Simple task (30 min): $40-60
- Medium task (1 hour): $60-80
- Complex task (2 hours): $80-120

Remember: People pay premium for speed. Don't undersell yourself!
