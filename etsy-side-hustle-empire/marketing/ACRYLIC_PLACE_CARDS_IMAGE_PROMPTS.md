## WHAT PERFORMS WELL ON ETSY (Research-Based Tips)

### For Images:

1. **First image is everything** - 90% of click decisions happen based on thumbnail
2. **Show the product in use** - Lifestyle shots convert better than plain product shots
3. **Bright, natural lighting** - Dark or yellow-tinted photos underperform
4. **Consistent style** - All 10 images should feel like a cohesive set
5. **Show scale** - Buyers need to understand actual size
6. **Feature all options** - If you offer white/gold/silver, show all three
7. **Square format** - Most consistent across Etsy's platforms

### For Videos:

1. **Hook in first 2 seconds** - Mobile users scroll fast
2. **Show the product immediately** - Don't waste time on logos or intros
3. **Movement attracts attention** - Etsy auto-plays videos on mobile
4. **No text needed** - Keep it purely visual
5. **Loop-friendly** - Design so the end flows into the beginning
6. **Bright and clear** - Dark videos don't perform as well

### Wedding Category Specifically:

1. **Emotional connection** - Show the romantic context
2. **Pinterest aesthetic** - Wedding buyers research heavily on Pinterest
3. **Show customization** - Personalized items are expected in weddings
4. **Quality signals** - Wedding purchases are high-stakes, show trustworthiness
5. **Seasonal timing** - Wedding content peaks Nov-May (engagement season through wedding season)

## PHOTOGRAPHY TIPS FOR YOUR OWN PHOTOS

If you're taking your own photos (recommended for authenticity):

**Equipment:**
- Smartphone with good camera (iPhone 12+ or equivalent)
- Natural light from a large window
- White foam board for bounce light
- Simple backgrounds (white marble contact paper, white linen)

**Settings:**
- Shoot in daylight (10am-2pm, indirect light)
- Clean your camera lens
- Use portrait mode for shallow depth of field
- Edit in Lightroom Mobile (free) for consistent color

**Props to Gather:**
- White napkins and plates
- Eucalyptus stems (fake or real)
- Small white flowers
- Gold flatware (thrift stores have these cheap)
- White candles
