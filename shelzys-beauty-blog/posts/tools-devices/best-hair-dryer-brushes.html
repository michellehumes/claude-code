Frequently Asked Questions

Can dryer brushes damage hair?

Less than a traditional blow dryer + round brush combo, since they require fewer passes. Use heat protectant and the medium heat setting.

How long does a blowout with a dryer brush last?

1-3 days depending on hair type. Sleep in a loose bun or silk bonnet to extend the life.

Are dryer brushes good for curly hair?

They can straighten and smooth curly hair beautifully. Start on medium heat and work in sections.
