Frequently Asked Questions

Do jade rollers actually work?

They help with temporary de-puffing through lymphatic drainage and feel soothing, but they don't change skin structure or reduce wrinkles.

Is microcurrent safe?

Yes. FDA-cleared devices like NuFACE are very safe. Avoid if you have a pacemaker, are pregnant, or have active skin conditions.

How often should I use a gua sha?

3-5 minutes daily for best results. Always use with a facial oil or serum to prevent dragging the skin.

Do I need both a jade roller and gua sha?

A gua sha is more versatile and effective. If you only get one tool, get the gua sha.
