Drunk Elephant vs The Ordinary: Can Budget Skincare Really Compete?

By Shelzy | 11 min read | Updated February 2026
Disclosure: This post contains affiliate links. If you purchase through these links, I may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. See my full disclosure.

Drunk Elephant is one of the most hyped luxury skincare brands. The Ordinary is the poster child for affordable, no-frills skincare. I swapped my Drunk Elephant products for their Ordinary equivalents for 8 weeks. Here's the truth about whether budget can compete with luxury.

Category-by-Category Comparison

Vitamin C Serum

DE C-Firma ($78)

  • 15% L-ascorbic acid
  • Pumpkin ferment extract
  • Fresh-mix technology
  • Elegant experience

TO Vitamin C 23% ($6)

  • 23% concentration
  • Suspended in silicone
  • Gritty texture
  • Incredibly affordable

Winner: DE C-Firma — The experience and formulation sophistication justify the price here. TO's texture is challenging.

Retinol

DE A-Passioni ($74)

  • 1% retinol
  • Vegan formula
  • Added peptides
  • Elegant cream texture

TO Retinol 0.5% ($6)

  • 0.5% retinol in squalane
  • Simple, effective
  • Lower concentration
  • Under $6

Winner: TIE — Both deliver retinol results. DE has higher concentration and fancier formula, but TO gets the job done for 92% less.

Moisturizer

DE Protini ($68)

  • 9 signal peptides
  • Unique bouncy texture
  • Visibly firms skin
  • Amino acid complex

TO Natural Moisturizing Factors ($8)

  • Amino acids + HA
  • No peptides
  • Basic but effective
  • Great value

Winner: DE Protini — The peptide complex genuinely delivers firming results that a basic moisturizer can't match.

Exfoliant

DE Babyfacial ($80)

  • 25% AHA + 2% BHA
  • Luxurious masking experience
  • Visible results one use
  • Pumpkin ferment

TO AHA 30% + BHA 2% ($8)

  • 30% AHA + 2% BHA
  • Higher acid concentration
  • Dramatic peeling mask
  • Under $8

Winner: The Ordinary — Higher concentration, similar results, fraction of the price. This is where TO truly shines.

Final Verdict

The Ordinary can replace about 60-70% of a Drunk Elephant routine with comparable results. Where DE truly justifies the premium: the C-Firma serum and Protini moisturizer. Where TO clearly wins: exfoliants and basic actives like retinol and niacinamide.

My strategy: Splurge on DE Protini and save with TO AHA/BHA Peeling Solution and TO Retinol.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Drunk Elephant worth the money?

For some products (Protini, C-Firma), the premium formulation delivers results that budget brands can't fully match. For others, The Ordinary performs just as well.

Are The Ordinary products safe?

Yes. The Ordinary products are well-formulated and tested. Start with lower concentrations if you're new to actives.

Can I mix Drunk Elephant and The Ordinary?

Absolutely. Mixing brands based on what works best is smarter than brand loyalty.
