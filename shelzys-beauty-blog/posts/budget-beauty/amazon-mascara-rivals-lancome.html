Frequently Asked Questions

How do I prevent Lash Princess from clumping?

Wipe the wand once on the tube opening before applying. Apply thin coats and wiggle from root to tip.

How long does the Essence Lash Princess last?

About 3-4 months before it starts drying out, which is standard for mascara. Replace every 3 months for hygiene.

Is Lash Princess cruelty-free?

Yes, Essence is cruelty-free and does not test on animals.
