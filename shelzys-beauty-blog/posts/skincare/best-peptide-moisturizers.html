Frequently Asked Questions

How long do peptides take to work?

Expect to see initial improvements in firmness and plumpness in 4-8 weeks. More significant wrinkle reduction takes 3-6 months.

Can I use peptides with retinol?

Yes! Peptides and retinol complement each other well. Use retinol at night and peptides morning or night.

What's the difference between peptides and retinol?

Retinol increases cell turnover and stimulates collagen via vitamin A pathways. Peptides signal collagen production via amino acid chains. Using both gives the best results.
