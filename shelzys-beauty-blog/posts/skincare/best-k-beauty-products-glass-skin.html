Frequently Asked Questions

What is glass skin?

Glass skin is a Korean beauty term for skin that's so hydrated and luminous it looks like glass — clear, dewy, and almost translucent.

How long does it take to achieve glass skin?

With consistent layering and hydration, you can see noticeable improvement in 2-4 weeks. Full glass skin typically takes 1-2 months.

Is snail mucin safe?

Yes, snail mucin is safe and well-tolerated. It's rich in glycoproteins, hyaluronic acid, and glycolic acid — all beneficial for skin.
