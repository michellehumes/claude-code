    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I prepare skin for self-tanner?

    Exfoliate 24 hours before, shave/wax 12 hours before, moisturize dry areas (elbows, knees, ankles) right before applying.

    How long does self-tanner last?

    5-7 days with proper prep and maintenance. Moisturize daily to extend the life of your tan.

    Will self-tanner make me break out?

    Quality face-specific formulas like Tan-Luxe are non-comedogenic. Avoid using body formulas on your face.
