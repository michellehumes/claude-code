Frequently Asked Questions

Do pimple patches work on cystic acne?

Standard hydrocolloid patches only work on surface-level pimples. For deep cystic acne, microneedle patches like ZitSticka are more effective.

Can I wear pimple patches under makeup?

Yes! Ultra-thin patches like the Mighty Patch Invisible+ and COSRX patches work well under makeup.

How long should I leave a pimple patch on?

At least 6 hours, ideally overnight. The patch turns white as it absorbs fluid — that's how you know it's working.
