Frequently Asked Questions

Should I apply HA to wet or dry skin?

Always damp skin! HA draws moisture from its environment — on dry skin in dry air, it can actually pull moisture FROM your skin.

Can you use too much hyaluronic acid?

Yes. Over-layering HA can make skin feel tacky. One thin layer on damp skin is sufficient.

Does HA help with wrinkles?

HA plumps skin temporarily, making fine lines less visible. It's hydrating, not anti-aging — pair with retinol for wrinkle reduction.
