Frequently Asked Questions

Can niacinamide cause purging?

Niacinamide doesn't typically cause purging since it's not an exfoliant. If you break out, you may be sensitive to the concentration — try a lower percentage.

Can I use niacinamide with vitamin C?

Yes! Despite old myths, niacinamide and vitamin C work beautifully together. Modern formulations are stable when combined.

What percentage of niacinamide is best?

2-5% is effective for most people. 10%+ is for oily skin or stubborn pores. Start lower if you have sensitive skin.
