    Frequently Asked Questions

    What ingredients matter most over 40?

    Retinol, vitamin C, peptides, hyaluronic acid, ceramides, and SPF. These are your core 6 ingredients.

    Is it too late to start a skincare routine at 40?

    Absolutely not. Skin responds to good ingredients at any age. You'll see improvements within weeks.

    How long before I see anti-aging results?

    Hydration improves immediately. Texture smooths in 2-4 weeks. Fine lines and spots improve in 6-12 weeks with consistent use.
