Frequently Asked Questions

Can beginners do skin cycling?

Yes! It's actually ideal for beginners because the recovery nights prevent the irritation that makes people quit actives.

Can I modify the cycle?

Absolutely. If 4 nights feels too frequent, extend to a 5-6 night cycle with more recovery nights.

Should I skin cycle in the morning too?

No. Skin cycling is an evening-only routine. Morning is for vitamin C, moisturizer, and SPF.
