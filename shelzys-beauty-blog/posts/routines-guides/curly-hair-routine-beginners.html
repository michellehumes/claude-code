Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I wash curly hair?

1-2 times per week. Curly hair is naturally drier and over-washing strips essential oils.

What's 'scrunching out the crunch'?

After gel dries and forms a cast, scrunch your hair with a tiny bit of oil to break the cast, revealing soft, defined curls.

How long does the curly hair transition take?

If you've been heat-styling for years, expect 3-6 months for your natural curl pattern to fully recover.
