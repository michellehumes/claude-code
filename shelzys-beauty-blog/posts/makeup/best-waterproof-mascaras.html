Frequently Asked Questions

How do I remove waterproof mascara without losing lashes?

Use an oil-based cleanser or micellar oil. Hold a soaked cotton pad on closed eyes for 30 seconds, then gently wipe. Never rub or tug.

Is it bad to wear waterproof mascara every day?

Daily use can dry out lashes over time. Consider alternating with regular mascara on low-key days.

Why does my waterproof mascara still smudge?

Usually it's oils from skincare transferring to lashes. Let eye cream fully absorb, and set under-eye area with powder before applying mascara.
