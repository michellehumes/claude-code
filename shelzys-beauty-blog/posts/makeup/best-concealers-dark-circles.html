Frequently Asked Questions

How do I prevent concealer from creasing?

Set with a translucent powder, use a hydrating eye cream first, and don't apply too much product.

Should I go lighter with under-eye concealer?

1-2 shades lighter than your skin tone brightens dark circles. Going too light creates a reverse-raccoon effect.

What's the best concealer application method?

Apply in an inverted triangle shape under the eye, then blend with a damp beauty sponge using bouncing (not swiping) motions.
