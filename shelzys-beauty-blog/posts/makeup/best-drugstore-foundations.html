Frequently Asked Questions

Do drugstore foundations perform as well as high-end?

In blind tests, many drugstore foundations match or outperform their expensive counterparts. The gap has narrowed significantly.

How do I find my shade online?

Most brands have online shade-matching tools. L'Oreal True Match even lets you match by undertone. Always check return policies.

Should I use primer with drugstore foundation?

A primer can extend wear time by 2-4 hours and help the foundation look smoother. But many of these perform well without one.
