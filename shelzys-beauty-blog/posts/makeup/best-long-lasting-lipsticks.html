Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make lipstick last even longer?

Exfoliate lips first, apply lip liner all over, then lipstick. Blot, dust translucent powder through a tissue, and apply a second coat.

How do I remove long-lasting lipstick?

Use an oil-based cleanser or micellar oil. Coconut oil works great in a pinch. Don't scrub — let the oil dissolve the formula.

Are liquid lipsticks bad for your lips?

Long-term daily wear of very matte formulas can be drying. Use a lip balm overnight and exfoliate weekly to keep lips healthy.
