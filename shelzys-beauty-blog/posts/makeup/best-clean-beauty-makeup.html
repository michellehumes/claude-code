Frequently Asked Questions

What does 'clean beauty' actually mean?

There's no legal definition, but clean beauty generally means free of parabens, phthalates, sulfates, synthetic fragrances, and other potentially harmful ingredients.

Is clean makeup as good as regular makeup?

Modern clean makeup has caught up significantly. Brands like ILIA and Kosas perform on par with conventional prestige brands.

Are all 'natural' products safe?

Not necessarily. Natural doesn't mean non-irritating. Poison ivy is natural. Always patch test new products regardless of their marketing.
