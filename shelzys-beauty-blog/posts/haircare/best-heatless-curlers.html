Frequently Asked Questions

Do heatless curlers work on short hair?

Rod-style curlers work best on shoulder-length or longer hair. For shorter hair, foam rollers or flexi rods are more effective.

How long do heatless curls last?

Typically 1-3 days depending on hair type. Use a light hairspray and sleep in a satin bonnet to extend the life.

Should hair be wet or dry for heatless curls?

Damp, not wet. About 80-90% dry hair gives the best results without taking forever to set.
