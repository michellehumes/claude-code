Final Verdict

For serious bond repair, it's a toss-up between Olaplex No. 3 and K18. K18 is faster and more dramatic; Olaplex is the proven classic. For severely damaged hair on a budget, Aphogee delivers professional-grade results for $12.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use bond-repair treatments?

Olaplex: weekly. K18: every wash for first 6 washes, then as needed. Aphogee: every 6 weeks maximum.

Can I use Olaplex and K18 together?

You can, but they work differently. Most people find one or the other sufficient. Try each separately first.

Do bond-repair treatments work on virgin hair?

Yes! Even undamaged hair has some broken bonds from sun exposure and environmental stress.
