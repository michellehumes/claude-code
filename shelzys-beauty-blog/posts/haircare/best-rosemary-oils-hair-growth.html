How to Use Rosemary Oil for Hair Growth

  1. Mix 3-5 drops of rosemary essential oil with 1 tablespoon of carrier oil (jojoba, coconut, or argan)
  2. Massage into scalp for 2-3 minutes
  3. Leave on for at least 30 minutes (or overnight)
  4. Shampoo out normally
  5. Repeat 3-4 times per week for 3-6 months

Frequently Asked Questions

How long until rosemary oil shows results?

The clinical study showed results at 6 months. Most users report noticing new growth at 3-4 months with consistent use.

Can rosemary oil regrow hair?

Research suggests it stimulates hair follicles similarly to minoxidil. It's most effective for thinning hair, not complete baldness.

Do I need to dilute rosemary essential oil?

Yes! Pure essential oils must be diluted with a carrier oil. The exception is pre-made blends like Mielle, which are already diluted.
