Frequently Asked Questions

Can thinning hair be reversed?

In many cases, yes — especially when caused by nutrient deficiency, stress, or hormonal changes. Genetic thinning can be slowed and managed but not fully reversed.

How long until scalp treatments show results?

Most treatments need 3-6 months of consistent use. Hair grows roughly 1/2 inch per month, so patience is essential.

Should I see a dermatologist for thinning hair?

If thinning is sudden, patchy, or accompanied by other symptoms, definitely see a dermatologist to rule out medical causes.
