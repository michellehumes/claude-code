Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I wash color-treated hair?

Every 2-3 days maximum. Overwashing is the #1 cause of premature color fading.

Does water temperature affect hair color?

Yes! Hot water opens the cuticle and releases color. Always rinse with cool water to seal color in.

When should I start using color-safe shampoo?

Immediately after coloring. Wait at least 48 hours before the first wash, then use color-safe shampoo exclusively.
