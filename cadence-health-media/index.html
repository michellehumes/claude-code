Services

What We Do

End-to-end HCP media strategy and execution for pharmaceutical and biotech brands.

HCP Media Strategy

Comprehensive media planning and buying strategies targeting healthcare professionals across endemic and non-endemic channels, optimized for reach, frequency, and clinical relevance.

Omnichannel Planning

Integrated media plans that unify programmatic, point-of-care, conference, and peer-to-peer channels into a cohesive physician engagement journey.

Analytics & Measurement

Rigorous performance analytics including Rx lift studies, NPI-level targeting, and real-time optimization to demonstrate clear ROI on media investments.

Launch Media Planning

Strategic media planning for new product launches, including pre-launch disease awareness, launch amplification, and post-launch optimization across therapeutic areas.

Vendor & Partner Management

Expert negotiation and management of media vendor relationships, ensuring optimal rates, premium placements, and transparent reporting across all partners.

Compliance & Best Practices

Media strategies built with regulatory compliance in mind, ensuring all HCP-directed communications meet industry standards and platform-specific requirements.
